Chunyang Chen 陈春阳

I am a lecturer (Assistant Professor) in Faculty of Information Technology, Monash University, Australia. My research focuses on Mining Software Repositories, Text Mining, Deep Learning, and Human Computer Interaction. I obtained my PhD from School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, and bachelor's degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT), China, 2014.
