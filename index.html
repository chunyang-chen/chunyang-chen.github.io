Chunyang Chen 陈春阳

I am a lecturer (Assistant Professor) in Faculty of Information Technology, Monash University, Australia. My research focuses on Software Engineering, Deep Learning and Human-Computer Interaction. I obtained my PhD from School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, under the supervision of Zhenchang Xing and Yang Liu. When pronouncing my first name, "Chun-yang" sounds like "train-young".
